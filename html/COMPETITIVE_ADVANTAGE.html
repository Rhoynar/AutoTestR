Competitive Advantage

Want to streamline your QA organization? It’s time to switch to Rhoynar.

As your software requirements grow, so should your QA infrastructure. Using old and antiquated technologies to do QA will only cause more problems than solve it. Off-shoring your QA work will only result in subpar product quality.

Why should you switch to Rhoynar Software Consulting?

  • Manual Test

    If you are using manual testing in your organization, then you already know the reason why you need to switch. Manual testing is tedious, error-prone and time consuming. Not to mention the amount of money spent on repeating regression tests on minor changes.

    • Faster execution cycle – Once you have developed the test cases, automation tests can complete 95% faster than corresponding manual tests.
    • No costs after development – Manual tests cycles cost the same whether they are run once or 100th time. Whereas Automation tests are virtually free once developed and deployed. The costs of manual to automation testing are recouped within the first year itself providing a huge return on investment.
    • Reliable – Automation Testing does not suffer from human errors and is therefore more reliable in catching issues.
    • Versatile – There are many types of tests which cannot be done manually. Usually manual is suitable for initial exploratory testing but after that point, any organization should be using automation to complete other types of testing.
  • HP QTP

    Why you should switch from HP QTP to Selenium based solution?

    • Nothing to purchase/nothing to license: Selenium is 100% open-source and free to use. Unlike HP QTP which will lock you in their ecosystem with huge licensing fees annually for each QA engineer.
    • Support for multiple browsers, multiple languages and multiple operating systems: Selenium supports all major browsers, and has language bindings so you can write your test cases in any language.
    • Faster Execution Cycle: Selenium Execution cycle is in some cases 80% more faster than corresponding tests in QTP.
    • Continuous Integration: Selenium is easily integrated with open source CI systems like Jenkins that would allow for a continuous delivery cycle reducing your development cycle time.
    • Rhoynar can help you do the migration to Selenium and Train your QA team on Selenium to make continued improvements to your software products.
  • Off-shore QA

    Why you should switch from off-shore QA solutions to Rhoynar’s automation solution?

    • Rhoynar’s on-shore team would work with you directly to come up with customized solutions for your issues. There is no language/cultural barriers that you would face with offshore company.
    • Accountability: Our team of QA experts are responsible professionals who are hand picked after thorough screening and interview process. Rhoynar Software Consulting makes sure that project times and deadlines can never be missed.
    • Cost effective and value adding: Let’s face it: off-shore products sub-par quality. You get what you pay for. Whereas Rhoynar Software Consulting utilizes innovative technologies such as AutoTestR ® and MigrateR® which provide effective, robust testing solutions at lower costs than off-shoring solutions.
    • Automation and Continuous Integration: Off-shore companies provide a test suite which is suitable for your website today but they would not setup a continuous integration system for you. Rhoynar’s Continuous Integration solution automates the whole process for change management – from check-in and unit tests to integration and acceptance tests
  • In House QA Department

    Do you have in-house QA department? Rhoynar Software Consulting can help you here too!

    • In house training: Rhoynar Software Consulting would provide training to your QA department through our sister arm at DestinationJ Software Technologies. This way, your employees would have the training available for latest technologies and best practices in software testing and would be able to utilize these practices for adding value to the company.
    • Best practices review: What are the tools your company is using? What are the methodologies it is using for different types of testing? Rhoynar Software Consulting can help make this more efficient and effective for the entire QA organization.
    • Migration from Manual/QTP to Selenium: Rhoynar Software Consulting would review and help your QA organization to migrate from manual and/or QTP test cases to Selenium WebDriver based automation test cases.
    • Setup Continuous Integration: Rhoynar would help setup continuous integration in your organization either on your intranet or through a cloud based test services provider. This will add more value in allowing for more testing on more platforms and devices and faster turnaround cycles.
    • Cost effective and value adding: The value brought in by pointing the QA organization in the right path saves money in the long run and helps keep your products robust and secure.
  • Continuous Integration

    Rhoynar’s CI solutions are based on the latest open source models for continuous integration.

    • Full automation: Rhoynar would provide 100% automation belt for your software change management. We are experienced with setting up automation in many major organizations with variety of different requirements. Our solutions would provide one-click change management solutions that would run the change through a set of automation jobs including pre-flight verification, regression validation, integration validation etc. There is no guesswork involved anymore on if any change would break during production.
    • In house training: Rhoynar would provide continuous integration training to your QA department through our sister arm at DestinationJ Software Technologies. This way, your employees would have the training available for latest technologies and best practices in software testing and would be able to maintain and add tests going forward.
    • Cost effective and value adding: The value brought in by automation and continuous integration allows to catch bugs early and adds value to the organization in the long run.
